I am disappearing for one week, to a secret country retreat &lt;scottish accent&gt;known as _the meadows_&lt;/scottish accent&gt;, to fast and pray, and wait. I will be offline in every sense of the word.

I'm also dismantling this site again, again daring myself to recode it. It probably will not be reassembled before I come back, but hopefully soon thereafter.

Waiting. Yes, that is appropriate this time of year. 

> ...when we stop and do nothing we begin to traverse the long distance between the head and the heart. we begin to notice our experience of life - the motivations and forces that drive our activities. it is difficult to stop and sit still before god...

-- [Johnny Baker](https://jonny-baker.com)
