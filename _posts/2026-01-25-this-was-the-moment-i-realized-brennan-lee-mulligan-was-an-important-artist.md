[During the weeks I couldn't move or really think](/2026/01/10/how-to-horribly-end-a-year/) I had a lot of time to kill and ended up getting into the new [Critical Role](https://critrole.com) season [on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@criticalrole). I'd tried to get into their stuff a few years ago but didn't. But once I got about half an episode into [the new season](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL1tiwbzkOjQxygnACZdKLhzTTB3CoM8gi), I was hooked, and burned through the backlog of 10 episodes[^1] before I was back into a semi-functional state. And now I'm a huge fan, watching every official or fan video YouTube throws at me.[^2]

I was already a fan of [Brennan Lee Mulligan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brennan_Lee_Mulligan)'s work since before CollegeHumor became Dropout, although I'd never watched his <abbr title="Table Top Role Playing Game">TTRPG</abbr> stuff. But I'm confident part of why I'm so into _this_ season of Critical Role is because he's <abbr title="Dungeon Mastering">DMing</abbr>. There are subtle but powerful through-lines in his worldbuilding and storytelling. But it wasn't until I watched this video that I realized his work is what I would categorize as _important_.[^3]
