<b>Update on Direction:</b>
<br />As our art makes more and more of a social commentary and our hearts turn in the direction of social action, we are looking for avenues that allow us to continue our art in this way. That is, specifically, to bring about broader change through the seeking of story and beauty and the formation of meaning through the same. Even more specifically, to bring optimism to pessimistic circumstances, hope to seemingly hopeless circumstances, and a new economy to those for whom this active one fails time and again. And to put a final point on the nail, our desire is to see Slovakia, Miriam's homeland, rejuvinated spiritually in these manners as it continues to struggle against the western economy and the adjusting of its value system.
<br />
<br />We also desire to be able to facilitate others' similar pursuits and be able to equip them in whatever manner they require such.
<br />
<br />"I am an evangelist for the arts." -daniel
