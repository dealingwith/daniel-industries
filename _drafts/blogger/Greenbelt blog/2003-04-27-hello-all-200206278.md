I've been actively lurking (if that term can be applied to blogs) here for a while, and since I was signed in to Blogger (not a frequent occasion) thought I'd drop my first line here.
<br />
<br />I have a question: am I the only American contrib'r to this blog? Maybe I should attempt to focus on that voice, although honestly I'm not <em>very</em> American, in the typical sense of the word. And in two days I'm moving to Sarajevo, Bosnia.
<br />
<br />My "trip" to Greenbelt has many facets this year. Last year I decided to come about one week out, and was really helped out by the <a href="http://freshworship.org/">Grace</a> people, who I had met briefly during a prior trip to London.
<br />
<br />This year I am <em>definately</em> coming, if I have to swim there. But I am also looking to perform, hopefully at the Performance Cafe venue. I was also asked if I wanted to do something for New Forms 1 or 2. I definately have some ideas for NF2, I don't have the people power for something in NF1, but I will have to wait until we're settled in Sarajevo to again start thinking about that.
<br />
<br />I am looking to strike that perfect balance between participating as a creative and also as a consumer, having it as a venue but not allowing that creative activity overwhelm the getting-something-out-of-it-all. It is the highlight of my artistic calendar either way.
