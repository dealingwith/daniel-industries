Ah, yes, what goes around comes around. This from <a href="http://www.relevantmagazine.com/">relevant mag</a>:
<br />
<br />"The city of Cypress, Calif., condemned 18 acres belonging to Cottonwood Christian Center last week, in order to convey the land to warehouse club Costco. The church, spiritual home to more than 4,000 families, bought the land in Sept. 1999. The property was then permitted for a 108,000 square foot office building, though the zoning in place did allow for church uses. The city staff told the church that it preferred retail on the site, but the church went forward with its $14 million purchase. The city's position is blunt: It wants the tax revenue that Costco will bring and is low-balling the church on the value of the land. Yes, lawsuits are flying..."
