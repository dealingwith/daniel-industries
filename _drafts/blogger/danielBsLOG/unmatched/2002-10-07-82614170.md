saw Emily & Susan tonight at the open mic showcase at Whitlows. i've never seen a local act so awe-inspiring. i've seen good bands, yes; but no act where you are left wondering how they are possibly not signed to a major label. they held the entire room captive. no one spoke for the entire hour; i've only seen that before with, like, famous people. they've got that gift. i wish there were some songs i could point you to; i'm lucky enough to have the 2 song demo in mp3 on my computer. if you want a listen email me and i'll email u back a single.
