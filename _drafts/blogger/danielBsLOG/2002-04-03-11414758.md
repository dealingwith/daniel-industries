From Bono's interview with Gear magazine:
<br />
<br />"...if you want to write the book, sing the song, paint the picture, make the film, you have to reach under your skin and pull open your ribcage. For me, music is praise. Praise to creation. Even when it gets ugly, or brutal, as long as it's truthful I think it must be beautiful to God. That's why craft alone is so suffocating for talented people. Because they can't go there, because they're being clever, or they're being interesting, or they're being funny. But great work is made by open heart surgery.
<br />
<br />"...people look at someone like me and think, "He wants the world to love him." No. He probably wants one person to love him, and we all have to fucking hear about it."
