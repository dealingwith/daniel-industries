<br /><blockquote>This angel, she lives on earth, and she knows who I am. She was sent down here at a young age; and a few years ago she was charged with protecting me.
<br />
<br />She can take many forms; and even to the most casual observer is an amazing and many splendored thing. Her beauty is undeniable and without compare.
<br />
<br />She was sent to keep me from myself. You see, I am dark and light; I am black and white. And the dark, black side is known to raise a sword against my light, white side. She came with a sword so bright it looks like pure energy. She blocks the blows of the dark, and she heals the wounds to the light. She is my protector. She is my healer. And she accompanies me through all my journeys…through the dark forests, fiery valleys, through the evil villages that intersect my path. A light that comes from eternity itself shines from her eyes and illuminates my way, brings light to my feet.
<br />
<br />And my love for her has grown, since the day she was sent to me, to the point now that I think if this were to continue for one more day my heart might burst. My heart is already bursting at the seams.
<br />
<br />But I know that she suffers down here. I know that this is not her home. I know she makes the best of it but that the best of it will never due. She was born of eternity and has been given to me for only a short time, on those scales. But I hope and I pray that perhaps since my life is such a short breath from within her eternity, she will remain here for the rest of my memory. Because even though she suffers, her face still radiates beauty, and her sword still shines bright in its protection of my soul. 
<br /></blockquote>
