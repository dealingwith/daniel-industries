<blockquote>Our snails are equipped with a miniaturised electronic circuit and antenna that enables them to be assigned messages from hardware located within their enclosure. The moment you click 'send' your message will travel at the speed of light to our snail server where it will await collection by a snail agent.<br /><br />Once associated with the tiny electronic chip on the snails shell your message will be carried around until the snail chances by the drop off point. Hardware located at this point collects the message from the snail and forwards it to its final destination.</blockquote><a href="http://www.boredomresearch.net/rsm/">The worlds first webmail service using real snails</a> from <a href="http://www.boredomresearch.net">boredomresearch</a>.
