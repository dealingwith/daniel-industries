As you may have already seen, the book is still a  work in progress, and we're always adding to and changing it. Once we finish it  and decide it's ready, we may publish it through <a href="http://www.cafepress.com/cp/info/sell/books.aspx">CafePress Publishing</a>.</blockquote><blockquote>In the summer of 2003, a fascinating socio-zoological development in the MetaUrchin sub-civilization    occurred. No cyberscape was left    unsullied, no stone was left unturned, no Flash was left undecompiled. And what    was the clarion call that beckoned these fair travelers to their voracious feedings?    Sustenance? Wealth? Manifest Destiny? No. They came to play an Alternate Reality    Game.</blockquote>
