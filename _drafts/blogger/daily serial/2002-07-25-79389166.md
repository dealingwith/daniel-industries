<center><b>Act V</b></center>
<br />
<br />SCENE: <i>Back in the forest, next to the pond. ROB has his loot spread out on the ground. He’s built a lean-to and is trying to start a fire.</i>
<br />
<br />ROB: Damn the Boy Scouts! How does this work?
<br />
<br />{He works without success with the flint and steel to light a small pile of green leaves and twigs.}
<br />
<br />Oh, screw the fire.
<br />
<br />{He grabs a loaf of bread and begins to eat. He eats about four large bites.”
<br />
<br />Man, I’m full. I need a drink.
<br />
<br />{He drinks from the pond and sits down again. He grabs the machete from the ground, leans back against a tree and stares hypnotically into the reflections in the machete.}
<br />
<br />You’re ok. You’re ok. You have anesthetized your mind to the weirdness of your situation. You’re ok. You’ll make it. You’ll find help. A way out of here. Anything. Don’t worry about it.
