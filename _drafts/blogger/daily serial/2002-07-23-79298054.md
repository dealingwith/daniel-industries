<center><b>Act IV</b></center>
<br />
<br />SCENE: <i>Back on the beach . ROB walks on from stage right.</i>
<br />
<br />ROB: Shit! I’m back at this God-forsaken beach. What am I going to do on this island with no place to go and nothing to eat and only one place with fresh water. I’m doomed {pause} Hey! How long have I been here? I wonder why it hasn’t gotten any darker? Where am I? Yikes! Better not think about that anymore.
<br />
<br />{Rob plops down on the beach. He stares off into the sea. After a short time of this, he rises excitedly to his feet.}
<br />
<br />A miracle! A miracle! A miracle! Something! Anything!
<br />
<br />{ROB runs to stage right and pulls out a large chest. He drags it with a bit of trouble to center stage. He pries it open with relative ease and peers inside. He names the contents as he dumps them on the beach.}
<br />
<br />A musket?! Powder? Blankets! Bread! Tons of bread! Flint. Steel. A machete. Yes! This is the mother lode. I’m set!
<br />
<br />{ROB runs into the forest with his arms full of his bounty and humming “God Save the Queen.”}
