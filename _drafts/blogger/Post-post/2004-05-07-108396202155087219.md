<br /><em>
<br />The Polyphonic Spree
<br />Presented by The Dallas Observer
<br />American Airlines Plaza Stage
<br />Virtually overnight this 22 member group, led by former Tripping Daisy front man Tim DeLaughter, have defined a new music genre with their uplifting musical message that's catchy pop minimally laced with a smidgen of lively Godspell-like attitude thrown into the mix. Widely embraced by media and critics alike, the group boasts a ten-member choir, a pair of keyboardists, as well as a percussionist, bassist, guitarist, flautist, trumpeter, trombonist, violist, a French horn player, a theremin player, and an electronic effects wizard. Currently on tour with the legendary David Bowie, "the Spree" got the call of a lifetime recently when they were given the nod to co-headline the Lollapalooza Tour later this summer alongside The String Cheese Incident, The Flaming Lips, Morrissey, and Sonic Youth.</em>
