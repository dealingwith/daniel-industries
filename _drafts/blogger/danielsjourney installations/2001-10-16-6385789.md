<br />…dealing with personal and cosmic events of august-september, 2001…
<br />
<br />"Without knowing how to respond to these acts of abuse and hatred on both a micro and a macro scale, I immediately searched for meaning…
<br />
<br />"I'm convinced there is none. 
<br />
<br />"…There are only small connections we can make each day that keep scraps of sanity in our cupboards of life.

<br />"{This} is about my attempt at a few small connections."
<br />
<br />{Hate Mail} is a non-linear story. Through a combination of technology, photography, and text, participants experience various versions of the story based on their personal interaction with it. The live encounter with the story will introduce an additional variable as participants subliminally experience other’s stories before and after creating their own.
