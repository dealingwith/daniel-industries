<a href="http://www.fray.com/hope/2001/">{fray} hope - a year of stories</a> Two-thousand and one. Say it out loud and let your mind wander. A year ago you might have thought of Kubrick. Spaceships. The future.
<br />These days the phrase conjures up world-weary images. Bombs and bullets. Recession and unemployment. The 3-block hole in New York City where two buildings and thousands of lives should be.
