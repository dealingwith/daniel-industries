This is a nice way of putting it:
<br />
<br /><a href="http://www.truetunes.com/Content/Features/050800_altworshiphowto.asp">True Tunes -- Fresh Vital Worship</a> Worship is a wonderful gift, a response to having been found by God and being loved. It's a love language - holding it back is impossible. Some have found the traditional language and worship of the church passed down the centuries strikes a chord and enables them to articulate what's in their hearts. Others find singing choruses and hymns led by a band seems to be the key that opens up the windows of their hearts, a language they have come to know and love. Somewhere between these two groups are those who have been touched by the awesome presence of God but these two ways of expressing it leave them cold. They are either too formulaic, a bit one-dimensional, seem to be speaking a language from a different planet, or are just over familiar. For whatever reason they don't seem to resonate within. That's not to disparage those who have found the way to express what's on their hearts - after all, diversity reflects the creativity of God. But if that is you, you know.
